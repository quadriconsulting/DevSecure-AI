
# DevSecure RPS Risk Engine

## What is the RPS Risk Score?
The DevSecure Risk Prioritization Score (RPS) is an unbounded score used to prioritize vulnerabilities based on real-world risk signals.

## Risk signals used by the engine
The RPS engine correlates multiple intelligence sources including:
- public vulnerability databases
- exploit likelihood signals
- dependency exposure
- attack surface visibility
- code reachability

## Why risk prioritization matters
Many vulnerabilities have low exploitation risk. RPS helps teams focus on issues most likely to impact production systems.

## How developers use RPS
Security teams sort vulnerabilities by RPS to prioritize remediation work efficiently.

## How RPS improves remediation workflows
By highlighting high-risk vulnerabilities first, teams reduce mean time to remediation and address the most dangerous issues faster.
